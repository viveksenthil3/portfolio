Featured Projects

Enhancing Law-Enforcement Audio Transcription: LoRA-Based Whisper Adaptation

PyTorch • Transformers • LoRA • Whisper • ASR

MS Capstone Project (Advisor: Zhiqiang Tao) - Parameter-efficient fine-tuning of OpenAI Whisper using Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) to improve transcription accuracy for body-worn camera (BWC) footage with law enforcement-specific language and terminology.

  • Achieved 38.76% relative Word Error Rate (WER) reduction vs. zero-shot baseline (0.6194 → 0.3793)
  • Implemented LoRA with rank r=8 constraining weight updates to low-rank subspace, reducing trainable parameters to 294K (0.3% of full model)
  • Addressed out-of-vocabulary (OOV) problem for tactical codes, legal terminology, and regional law enforcement dialect
  • Supported by U.S. Department of Justice grant (15PBJA-22-GG-03328-BWCx)

Research Paper Abstract

"Towards AI-Driven Policing: Interdisciplinary Knowledge Discovery from Police Body-Worn Camera Footage"

Conference: 9th ASEBP Conference, Tucson, AZ

Abstract:

This research presents a multimodal system for analyzing police body-worn camera footage by integrating audio, video, and text processing capabilities. The system employs transformer-based architectures for speaker source separation and transcription, combined with advanced video analysis techniques. Key contributions include:

  • A transformer-based speaker source separation model that effectively isolates overlapping voices from raw audio footage
  • Integration of Whisper model for accurate audio transcription, ensuring reliable text extraction
  • Large-scale audio processing optimized for distributed GPU-accelerated computing platforms
  • Interdisciplinary framework combining AI/ML with law enforcement domain knowledge for actionable insights

Keywords: Multimodal AI, Speaker Diarization, Audio-Video Analysis, Natural Language Processing, Police Technology
